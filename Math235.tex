\item Inductively working on $A_1$,Notices that $\begin{vmatrix}\lambda & \vec{b}^T\\\vec{0}&A_1\end{vmatrix}$ is already upper triangular
\item We are looking for Orthogonal Matrix $Q$ such that $Q^TA_1Q = T_1$
\end{itemize}
